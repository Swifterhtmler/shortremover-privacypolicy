Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

ShortRemover is a Safari extension that hides YouTube Shorts from your browser. We take your privacy seriously.

Data Collection

ShortRemover does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app has no analytics, no tracking, and no servers.

Local Storage

The extension saves your on/off toggle preference locally on your device using the browser's built-in storage API. This data never leaves your device.

Third Parties

ShortRemover does not share any data with third parties because no data is collected in the first place.
